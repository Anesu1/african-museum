Art of Protest explores the creative work that sustained liberation movements, including posters, songs, poetry,
and street performance. The exhibition shows how artists amplified messages of hope and mobilized communities.

Workshops and talks accompany the gallery, inviting visitors to learn about visual storytelling and cultural
resistance across Africa.
